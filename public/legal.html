About Qopy

Qopy is a service for secure, temporary text sharing with client-side encryption. All shared content is encrypted in your browser before being transmitted to our servers, ensuring we never see your plain text content. All shared content is automatically deleted after the selected time period. No permanent copies or logs of content are stored. The service operates on a privacy-first basis with automatic data deletion and zero-knowledge architecture.
